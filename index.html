About This Game

Beetleball is a team-based PvP beetle soccer game where you score goals with dungballs.

TEAM UP WITH YOUR FRIENDS IN PVP BEETLE SOCCER

Teamplay and creativity matters just as much as mechanics. Experiment with beetle class team comps to climb ranks, or just mess around with friends in custom lobbies.

PICK YOUR MAIN FROM TONS OF UNIQUE CLASSES

Find the beetle that matches your playstyle with over a dozen beetle classes, each with abilities based on their real life species.

WIN GAMES WITH CRAZY BEETLE ABILITIES

Teleport from ball to ball with the Tiger Beetle, create mushroom launchpads as the Fungus Beetle, shoot ladybugs out of the air with the Cyborg Beetle's laser sniper, and much more! Cyborg beetles are real, ok?

THIS ISN'T A STANDARD SOCCER FIELD

Glide from dandelions to donuts, roll dungballs up cakes (gross), and headbutt enemies off of sandcastles. Beetleball's unique map design offers much more variety than standard FLAT and BORING soccer fields.

COMPETE FOR FRESHLY DROPPED DUNGBALLS

Merge many balls into one GOLDEN DUNGBALL to score. Multiple balls in play adds a new layer of team strategy beyond standard one ball soccer.

CLIMB THE RANKS WITH ADVANCED TECHNIQUES

Outsmart enemy beetles with class synergies, map tricks, and momentum plays to become the biggest and best beetle baller to ever roll dung.

BECOME A BEAUTIFUL BEETLE

Have you ever seen a beetle with a hat on? Now you have. Isn't it cute? With a bunch of cosmetic options like beetle skins, beetle hats, and beetle boots, you can become the beautiful beetle you've always longed to be.

FEATURES

  • Beetle soccer

  • Up to 6v6

  • Easy to get good, hard to master

  • Momentum-based movement

  • Multiple balls at once

  • 12+ unique classes

  • 8+ unique maps

  • Ranked + unranked matchmaking

  • Custom lobbies

  • Alternate game modes

  • Beetle hats!

If you're a fan of beetles, or just love balls, you'll love Beetleball!
